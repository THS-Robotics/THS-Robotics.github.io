ABOUT TAYLOR ROBOTICS

Taylor Robotics is a collection of engineers and programmers of various skill levels coming together in pursuit expanding our knowledge of STEM by way of robotics competitions. We compete in VEX competitions every year to take our prowess and ingenuity to their absolute limits. We believe in the following fundamentals-

Creativity

We strive to build the best robots we can. This means coming up with innovative ways to complete and accomplish tasks.

Collaboration

One is none; we are stronger together. Creating robots is a team effort, and we strive to encourage a positive teamwork environment to enhance our robotics experience.

Competition

Competitions are a testament to our skill. Whether we place high or score low, we can always learn from them.
