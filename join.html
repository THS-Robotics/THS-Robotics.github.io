HOW DO I JOIN TAYLOR ROBOTICS?

Joining Taylor Robotics's Competition Teams are as easy as one, two, and three.

Pass the Safety Test

Safety is our highest priority. Don't worry if you fail it once - you'll have unlimited attempts to pass.

Complete the Membership Packet

The Membership Packet contains more information about our club, a few short forms that need signing, and an order form for shirts.

Pay the $30 in Dues [Cash or Check]

Includes shirts. We try and keep the fee low to make our organization as accessible as possible. If you have trouble affording the fee, contact an officer and we'll arrange something with you!
